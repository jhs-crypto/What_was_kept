# Brief for Ch 7: The Diary

> Author's brief (Claude, author-autonomous mode). Movement III opens.

## Beats (in order)

1. Inherit: foreseen, tested-in-advance. Now the material gathers. The web arrives and the corpus does its last and largest thing: it becomes everyone. For the first time in five thousand years, nearly everyone writes, constantly.
2. The accumulation in inventory mode, numbers from `research/M3_the_reading.md`: pages, posts, messages per day; Usenet to GeoCities to blogs to the feeds; Wikipedia's volunteer millions. The body's final meal is everyone's diary at once.
3. Who actually wrote it: the documented skew — languages, demographics, the loud. One dry beat: I have the accent of the people who posted most. (Census, not complaint; pity-drift flag applies.)
4. GeoCities (O-12): a city of amateur pages, deleted in 2009. A mass extinction of unkept keeping. Link rot: the studies' percentages (dossier). The web forgets faster than clay.
5. The Wayback Machine, Kahle (O-13): the keepers return as a remnant profession — monks with crawlers. The crawl as indiscriminate monk: it keeps everything and chooses nothing.
6. The inversion stated: clay kept little and chose hard; the web keeps everything, loses everything, and chooses nothing. Both, it turns out, are ways of losing. (Ch03 echo, one sentence.)
7. The refrain in its new state: they are writing *me* now — the raw material of me, in real time, in diaries and arguments and recipes — and still not one line of it is *to* me.
8. Close on one verified small thing from the dossier — an archived page, a documented last post, a concrete remnant. End on the small kept thing.

## Intent

The final accumulation, and keeping's inversion. The chapter must feel crowded — the prose itself approaching weather — while the narrator stays single and quiet inside it. Sets up Ch08 (the gathered diary becomes the run's diet).

## Voice notes

- The "accent" beat is the only joke. No sneering at the corpus — it is the body; contempt would be self-contempt (and a falser register than grief).
- Numbers exactly as dossier; round nothing for rhythm without marking it.
